ASSET FUSION

We believe an NFT project should be treated as a long-term investment, which provides holders with value over time.Our Asset Fusion feature not only reduces the original total supply, but also gives our most loyal holders a one of a kind NFT they can keep for their entire journey with us.

Collectors who mint 3 or more of our NFTs will be invited to the ASSET FUSION session, which will require them to burn one NFT, rewarding them with a one of a kind 3D version of their favourite mobster. This will form a new collection called EBM3D.
